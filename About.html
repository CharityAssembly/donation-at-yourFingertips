About Us

The Charity Assembly is a web based platforme that is created by two Gerogre Washington University's Integreated Information Science and Technology students- Getinet B-Alaemayehu and Suzan Abdalla. The website contains 100 charity organizations, and the mission of the website is to act as a hub for charities worldwide so, that doners can access this website from their computers, tablets, and smartphones at anytime from anywhere so that they can learn about these variety of organizations and make contributions towards the cause of their choice.
